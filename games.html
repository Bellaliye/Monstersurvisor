About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must defend yourself against endless waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, discover powerful weapons, and face increasingly challenging enemies.

The game features a simple yet addictive gameplay loop: survive, level up, and become stronger to survive even longer. Each run is unique, with randomly generated enemy patterns and power-up opportunities.

Inspired by classic arcade games and modern roguelite mechanics, Monster Survivors offers a challenging experience that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking. How long can you survive in this monster-infested world?

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies with your equipped weapon
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when you level up
  • Survive as long as possible!
